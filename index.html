In Loving Memory Of Bella Davis

Dear Sweet Bella

Missing You

Who knew a soulmate Could be wrapped in fur You were truly a best friend to me I will miss you in the morning Being the first that I’d see I will miss our long walks And our daily talks Although I was the one yapping away By my side is where you’d always stay With my hand patting your head t felt like you could hear every word I said I will miss everything about you How you would jump up against me And suddenly you were so tall Or your excited eyes waiting to catch the ball I will miss this and everything in between I hope you somehow knew You were truly my all

Bella
